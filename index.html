Ryan Jancula is a luxury real estate broker and principal and founder of the Jancula Group specializing in the luxury markets of Los Angeles. With a focus on luxury and ultra-luxury Homes, Estates, and New Construction properties, he also specializes in off-market and ocean-view homes as well. Ryan has been involved in over $750M in closed real estate transactions, including over $250M sold in just the last 24 months.

The Jancula Group provides buyers and sellers with a modern, innovative approach to real estate while also maintaining traditional values and the utmost integrity. The group has proven to work tirelessly to provide their client’s desired outcomes and are focused on creating long-term relationships above all else. Regularly recognized by publications such as the Wall Street Journal, Forbes, National Association of Realtors® 30 under 30, HousingWire, The Real Deal, and Los Angeles Magazine, Ryan and his group have quickly become one of the most accomplished and fastest growing teams in the nation.
